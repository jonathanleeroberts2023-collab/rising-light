My Story

Guided by Lived Experience

I know what it feels like to be lost — to think there is no way through. I also know what it feels like to find your footing, to breathe again, to rediscover who you truly are beneath the pain.

Based in Monroe, Michigan, I serve as a spiritual advisor, life coach, and recovery mentor for individuals throughout Southeast Michigan who are ready to reclaim their lives. My approach blends spiritual depth, practical coaching, and compassionate accountability — tailored to wherever you are right now.

What I Offer

Sessions Designed for Your Journey

Every person's path is unique. My services meet you where you are — whether you're in crisis, in recovery, seeking clarity, or simply ready to grow.

🕊️

Spiritual Guidance

Reconnect with your inner wisdom, explore your spiritual nature, and build a practice that grounds and sustains you through life's storms. All paths and beliefs are welcomed here.

🧭

Life Coaching

Set clear intentions, break limiting patterns, and create a roadmap for the life you want. We work together on goals, mindset, relationships, purpose, and daily habits.

💚

Addiction Recovery Support

Peer-based mentorship from someone who has been there. Accountability, encouragement, and real talk — because recovery is a journey, not a destination. Clean is possible.

👪

Family & Loved Ones

Addiction and struggle affect the whole family. I offer support and guidance for loved ones navigating how to help without enabling — how to love without losing yourself.

🌱

Group Sessions

There is power in shared experience. Small group sessions create community, reduce isolation, and remind us that none of us is alone on this path.

📞

Crisis Check-Ins

Sometimes you need someone right now. I offer check-in availability for clients in active recovery who need a grounding voice in difficult moments.
